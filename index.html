About Me

My name is Daniel and I am 16. Ever since I was 11 I had a passion for backend developing, as I was considered quite young, I was not able to join any course so I ended up learning Java through YouTube videos. However, it was not until I was 13 when I started really improving as I was given the chance to join a "developer trainee" course. This programme was offered by an online community and you got the chance to be taught by professional developers and after you proved yourself, to work side by side with them. From that moment on, my enthusiasm for software development has grown and grown and as a result my coding, team-working and logical reasoning abilities have prominently improved.
In my free time I enjoy coding (especially Bukkit plugins), cooking, reading, swimming, playing video games, doing taekwondo and hanging out with friends.
